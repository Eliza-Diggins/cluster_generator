cluster_generator#

cluster_generator is a cross-platform galaxy cluster initializer for N-body / hydrodynamics codes. cluster_generator supports a variety of different possible configurations for the initialized galaxy clusters, including a variety of profiles, different construction assumptions, and non-Newtonian gravity options.

Features#

Gravities

Profiles

  • Newtonian Gravity
  • AQUAL (MONDian)
  • QUMOND (MONDian)
  • [T/S]-NFW
  • Hernquist [cored / uncored]
  • Einasto
  • Power Law
  • Beta Model
  • am06
  • Vikhlinin (density)
  • Vikhlinin (temperature)
  • Entropy [baseline, broken, walker]
